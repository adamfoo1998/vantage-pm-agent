## Roadmap Implications for Vantage

### 1. Robinhood EU perps on gold + FX: Direct competitive strike — act now `[HIGH PRIORITY]`
Robinhood EU launched TradFi perps on gold, EUR/USD, oil, and ETFs **yesterday (July 1, 2026)** under MiCA/MiFID II. Their leverage cap is 10×. Vantage's FCA-regulated CFD offering (max 20× retail, up to 200× for professional) has a leverage advantage against Robinhood. The threat is brand recognition and the Robinhood UX pulling retail EU traders. Vantage should monitor whether Robinhood's gold perp gains traction as a XAUUSD alternative — and consider whether a USDT-margined perp wrapper would be additive for Vantage's crypto-native customer segment.

### 2. Binance 29-asset TradFi perp roster expanding rapidly `[MONITOR]`
Binance added ~27 TradFi assets in ~6 months after gold/silver. The Cerebras (CBRS) IPO perpetual shows Binance is willing to list individual-stock perps on hot IPOs with minimal lag. If Vantage's CFD launch covers the same underlying assets, Binance's 50× leverage vs Vantage's (presumably FCA-capped) leverage is a ceiling to be aware of for retail. Vantage's regulatory moat is quality of regulation, not leverage; messaging must reflect that.

### 3. Bybit and Bitget both offering 500× FX leverage `[REGULATORY RISK — VERIFY]`
Both are Mauritius FSC entities offering 500× leverage on FX (gold leverage tier for Bitget confirmed at 500×; for Bybit FX confirmed, gold tier `Inferred`). Vantage's regulatory positioning (FCA, ASIC) cannot match this leverage but should be framed as a trust and stability advantage, particularly for institutional and high-net-worth clients. `VERIFY`: Does Vantage have any Mauritius FSC entity that could offer higher leverage in appropriate jurisdictions?

### 4. Robinhood Chain + 2,000 stock tokens: tokenized RWA is real and live `[PATH B / RWA ROADMAP]`
Robinhood has launched tokenized equity at scale (2,000+ tokens on its own L2). This is the most advanced tokenized stock offering among competitors. For Vantage's tokenized RWA roadmap — if pursuing Path B (StarPrime / third-party oracle) — the Robinhood model (own chain + broker integration) represents the highest-bar benchmark. The SpaceX/SPCX token is specifically live on Robinhood EU. Vantage targeting SPCX CFD coverage would differentiate if delivered before competitors copy it.

### 5. Moomoo US FX gap is a retention opportunity `[SHORT-TERM]`
Moomoo US has no FX product. Any Moomoo US customer who wants FX has to go elsewhere. If Vantage markets FX + equities as a bundle, this is an addressable gap. Moomoo SG does have FX (36 pairs, 20× MAS) so the SG market is contested.

### 6. Bitstamp crypto perps (10 coins, EU retail) `[MONITOR]`
Bitstamp by Robinhood is targeting EU retail with regulated crypto perps. This is a signal that the institutional brand (Bitstamp) is now being used for retail products — a reversal of its traditional positioning. `VERIFY`: Does Vantage have an EU entity offering crypto perps? If not, this is a gap.
